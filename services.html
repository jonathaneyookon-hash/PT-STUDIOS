WHAT I OFFER

Livestream coverage built around your kind of event.

Every event has a different rhythm — a boardroom is not a wedding, and a wedding is not a church service. Coverage is planned around what actually matters for your event, not a one-size-fits-all setup.

01 — CORPORATE

Corporate Events

Conferences, product launches, AGMs, panel discussions, and internal town halls — streamed cleanly for remote stakeholders, press, and shareholders who need to see and hear everything clearly, on time.

02 — SOCIAL

Social Events

Weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, burials, and family celebrations. Streamed so relatives abroad or unable to travel still feel like they're in the room — not watching from a distance.

03 — RELIGIOUS

Religious Events

Church services, crusades, dedications, and ministry programs — streamed reliably every time, for congregations who depend on watching from home.
